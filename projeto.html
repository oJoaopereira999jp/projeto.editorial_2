In this publication we seek to find the duality between post-digital and techno solutionism.
Despite understanding that when we speak of post-digital we’re actually refering to the times that we currently live in:
the age where technology usage comes as an antecipated resource and as inteligent proccess in order to respond to our needs.
Techno solutionism is the reflexion and natural response of these exact times.
Which bears the question: Does technology solve all our problems?
